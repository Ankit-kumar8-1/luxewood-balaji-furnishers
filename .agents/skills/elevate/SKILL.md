# Elevate — Max-Effort Fresh-Eyes Re-examination

Take completed work, features, designs, or decisions and **re-examine them with maximum intelligence and fresh, unpolluted eyes**, hunting specifically for the *upside and missed opportunities* that a baseline pass walked past.

## Core Purpose

Where traditional review hunts for **what is wrong or broken**, `/elevate` hunts for **what was missed or could be taken to the next level**:
- The novel opportunity
- The non-obvious connection to another part of the system
- The high-value refinement or feature addition
- The aesthetic or architectural level-up

## Guidelines

1. **Re-examine without bias:** Look at finished code or design as if seeing it for the first time.
2. **Look for non-obvious wins:** Identify places where small, clever adjustments yield huge quality or performance gains.
3. **Elevate Aesthetics & UX:** Check if micro-interactions, animations, spacing, or visual hierarchy can be elevated from "good" to "extraordinary".
4. **Actionable Suggestions:** Provide concrete, actionable proposals rather than vague feedback.
